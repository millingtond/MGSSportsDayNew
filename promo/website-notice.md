## 1. News post

### Follow MGS Sports Day 2026 — live

Sports Day returns on **Wednesday 1 July 2026**, and this year there is a brand-new way for the whole MGS community to follow every result as it happens. Throughout the morning, 32 forms across Years 7 to 10 will go head to head in track and field — from the 100m, 200m, 300m, 800m and 1500m to the relays, plus high jump, long jump, javelin and shot. With **44 standing school records** up for grabs, there is plenty to play for.

New for 2026, we have replaced the old spreadsheet-and-typing routine with a modern **live results scoreboard**. Prefects record the result of each event — on the track and in the field — on their phones, and the moment the results tent confirms a result it appears on the public board within moments. Nothing is shown until it is confirmed, and the standings are always recomputed from confirmed results — so what you see is always accurate.

Wherever you are, you can keep up with how your son's form is doing. The board updates in real time and builds to a suspense-and-reveal finale, when the champions are announced around the end of the morning with a celebratory flourish.

**On the board you will find:**

- The whole-school table **and** a table for each year group (Years 7–10), all updating in real time
- Record-breaking call-outs as the 44 records come under threat
- A "now & next" timetable of what's coming up on the track and in the field
- A suspense → reveal finale crowning the champions, with confetti to finish

**Follow the live standings here:** [https://mgssportsday-55624.web.app](https://mgssportsday-55624.web.app)

No login needed — it works on any phone, laptop or computer.

*The PE Department*
