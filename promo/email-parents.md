# Email to parents / guardians

**Subject:** Follow MGS Sports Day live this year — Wednesday 1 July

Dear Parents and Guardians,

Sports Day is one of the proudest mornings in the MGS calendar, and this year you can share in it wherever you are.

On **Wednesday 1 July**, all 32 forms across Years 7 to 10 compete on the track and in the field, with 44 school records up for grabs. New for 2026, you can follow the whole morning live on our online scoreboard:

**https://mgssportsday-55624.web.app**

As each result is confirmed — on the track and in the field — the whole-school and year-group standings update in real time, so you can keep an eye on how your son's form is doing throughout the morning, right up to the champions reveal at the close. It works on any phone, tablet or computer, with no app to download and no login. (It shows live results and standings, not a video stream.)

Wishing all our boys a brilliant Sports Day.

With warm regards,
