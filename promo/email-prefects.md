# Email to the prefect results team

*A short pre-event briefing. The full step-by-step how-to is in [print/prefect-instructions.md](../print/prefect-instructions.md).*

**Subject:** Sports Day results team — your quick briefing for Wed 1 July

Hi all,

Thank you for being on the results team. On **Wednesday 1 July** you will record results on your phone — on the track and in the field — a few taps, no typing of times or distances.

**At your station**

1. Scan the QR code on your clipboard — or go to **https://mgssportsday-entry.web.app** and enter the short code on the sheet.
2. Tap your name once.
3. After each race or field event, tap the form cards in finishing order (1st, 2nd, 3rd...), then **Submit** — wait for the green **"Saved ✓"**.

**Good to know**

- **No signal?** Results save on your phone and sync when you are back in range — just find signal before you leave the field.
- **Made a mistake?** Reopen the event and tap **"Submit a correction"**; the latest version counts.
- **Phone dead or won't scan?** Use the paper backup sheet and hand it to the Results Tent.

Full step-by-step instructions will follow.
