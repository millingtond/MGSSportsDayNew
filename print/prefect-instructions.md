# Sports Day — entering results on your phone (prefect instructions)

*Copy/paste into an email to the prefects. Entry app: https://mgssportsday-entry.web.app*

---

**Subject: Sports Day — entering results on your phone**

Hi all,

On Sports Day you'll record race results on your phone. It's quick — a few taps, no typing of times. Here's all you need to do.

**Getting started (once)**
1. At your station, **scan the QR code** on the clipboard with your phone's camera (or go to **mgssportsday-entry.web.app** and type in the short code on the sheet).
2. **Type your full name** when asked — this stays on your phone, and lets us check back with you if there's ever a query about a result.

**For each race**
3. The app shows **the races at your station** — tap the one that's just finished. *(If it's not listed, tap the year group → event → race instead.)*
4. **Tap the form cards in the order they finished** — 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and so on. Tap **Undo** if you tap the wrong one.
5. Tap **Review**, check the order looks right, then **Submit result**. You'll see a green **"Saved ✓"**.

**Good to know**
- 📶 **No signal? No problem.** Your result is saved on your phone straight away and sends by itself when you're back in range. If you see "*X waiting to sync*", just find signal before you leave the field.
- ✏️ **Made a mistake after submitting?** Open that race again and tap **"Submit a correction"** — your latest version is the one that counts.
- 📄 **Phone not working?** Use the **paper backup sheet** at your station and hand it to the Results Tent.

That's it — thank you for helping run the day!
