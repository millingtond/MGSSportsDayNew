# Sports Day 2026 — Staffing & Roles (context)

Reference for who is doing what on the day. This is operational context for the
platform (prefect attribution, station naming, year/form groupings); it is not
loaded by the apps. Source: school's Sports Day prefect briefing.

## Event basics

- **Date:** Wednesday 1 July 2026
- **Meet time:** 08:20
- **Start time:** 09:00
- **Meet point:** Hibbitt Centre Dining Hall
- The Senior Officer Team and the Gold Prefects assist with running the event.

## Venue layout (see `Sports Day 2026 - Map.pdf`)

- **Infield / left field area:** Javelin (run-ups A, B, C), Shot (A, B, C), Relay
  Briefing Area, Band, Podium, Benches, the "Sports Day Hub" (WWW.), and speakers
  down the inside of the track.
- **Long Jump:** top-left, outside the track.
- **High Jump:** top-right, with its own High Jump Entrance; Toilets beyond it.
- **Track:** START on the home straight; markings for 100m, 200m, 300m/1500m; lanes.
- **Spectator side (right of track):** Year pens (7, 8, 9, 10), Refreshments, Fruit
  Stall, and First Aid.
