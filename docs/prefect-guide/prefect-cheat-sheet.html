Every race, step by step

  1. Open the app — scan the station QR (or type the code). Enter your name once at the start of the day.
  2. Pick the race — tap the suggested race, or choose year group › event › A/B/C.
  3. Tap the finishing order — winner first, then 2nd, 3rd… Wrong tap? Undo ↩ or the ✕.
  4. Check & Submit — tap any forms that didn’t run, add winning time if handy (optional), then Submit result.
  5. Wait for the green “Saved!” — that’s your proof it went in. Then “Enter another result”.

⭐ Golden rules

  • 📶No signal? Fine. It saves on your phone and sends itself later. Don’t refresh or delete.
  • ✅Always reach “Saved!” No green screen = it didn’t send.
  • ☝️One race at a time.
  • 🛟You can’t break it. The tent checks every result.
  • ✏️Mistake? “Submit a correction” — newest wins.
  • ❓Yellow card = the tent has a question. Tap, check, resend.
🏁

Before you leave the field: tap the ↻ to sync counter and make sure everything has sent. When it’s clear, you’re done — well played!

Questions on the day? Find the results tent or any member of staff. Thank you! 🏅
