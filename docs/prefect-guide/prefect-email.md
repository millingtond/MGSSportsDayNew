**Subject:** Sports Day scoring — your quick guide (it’s really easy!)

---

Hi everyone,

Thank you so much for helping with the scoring at **Sports Day on Wednesday 1 July** — you genuinely make the day run, and we’re really grateful.

Here’s the good news: recording results is **dead easy**. You’ll use your own phone, there’s nothing to download, and there are no passwords. When a race finishes you simply **tap the forms in the order they crossed the line and hit Submit**. That’s the whole job.

A few things worth knowing up front:

- **It works even with no signal.** Results save on your phone instantly and send themselves when you’re back in range — so don’t worry about patchy reception out on the field.
- **You can’t break anything.** The results tent checks and confirms every result before it counts, and corrections are always easy.
- **Always wait for the green “Saved!” screen** — that’s your proof a result has gone in.

I’ve attached two things:

1. **Prefect Scoring Guide (PDF)** — a short, picture-by-picture walkthrough of exactly what you’ll see and tap. Please have a quick skim before Wednesday (honestly, two minutes).
2. **One-page Cheat Sheet (PDF)** — print it and keep it at your station for a quick reminder on the day.

On the day, come to the **results tent** to get your station’s access code — or scan its **QR code** with your phone — then **enter your name once** and you’re away. If you get stuck at any point, the results tent or any member of staff will be glad to help.

Thanks again, and see you on the field!

Best wishes,
Mr Millington
